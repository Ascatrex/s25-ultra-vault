# Enable S Pen to text in all apps

> **Path:** Settings › Advanced features › S Pen › S Pen to text

## Why

Works across all apps — email, WhatsApp, browsers — making the S Pen universally useful.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Advanced features
3. Tap S Pen
4. Tap S Pen to text
5. Toggle on

> 💡 **Tip:** Double-tap the S Pen button while writing to delete the last word.
