# Configure Screen Off Memo

> **Path:** Settings › Advanced features › S Pen › Screen off memo

## Why

The fastest way to capture thoughts — no unlock, no app search, just pull and write.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Advanced features
3. Tap S Pen
4. Tap Screen off memo
5. Toggle on

> 💡 **Tip:** Pin the memo to your always-on display so you see it every time you glance at your phone.
