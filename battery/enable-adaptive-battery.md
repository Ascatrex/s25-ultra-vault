# Enable Adaptive Battery

> **Path:** Settings › Battery › Adaptive battery

## Why

Can add 2+ hours of screen-on time daily by intelligently managing background processes.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Battery
3. Tap Adaptive battery
4. Toggle on

> 💡 **Tip:** Takes 2 to 3 days to learn your patterns — battery life improves over the first week.
