# Enable Adaptive 120Hz refresh rate

> **Path:** Settings › Display › Motion smoothness › Adaptive

## Why

Adaptive drops to 1Hz when static, conserving battery without sacrificing smoothness.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Display
3. Tap Motion smoothness
4. Select Adaptive
5. Tap Apply
