# Disable app sleep for Galaxy AI apps

> **Path:** Settings › Battery › Background usage limits

## Why

App sleep can delay Galaxy AI responses and disable background transcription.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Battery
3. Tap Background usage limits
4. Tap Never sleeping apps
5. Add Galaxy AI and Samsung apps you rely on
