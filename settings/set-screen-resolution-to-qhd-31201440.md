# Set screen resolution to QHD+ 3120×1440

> **Path:** Settings › Display › Screen resolution › QHD+ 3120×1440

## Why

The 6.9-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display is stunning at QHD+ — text is sharper and photos more detailed.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Display
3. Tap Screen resolution
4. Select QHD+ 3120×1440
5. Tap Apply

> 💡 **Tip:** Battery impact is minimal on the S25 Ultra — the Snapdragon 8 Elite handles QHD+ efficiently.
