# Set up wireless DeX

> **Path:** Settings › Connected devices › DeX

## Why

Turn your S25 Ultra into a desktop with keyboard and mouse support over Wi-Fi.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Connected devices
3. Tap DeX
4. Toggle on Use DeX wirelessly
5. Select your Samsung Smart TV or Miracast display from the list

> 💡 **Tip:** Pair a Bluetooth keyboard and mouse for a full desktop experience.
