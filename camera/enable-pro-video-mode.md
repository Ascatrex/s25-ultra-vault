# Enable Pro Video mode

> **Path:** Camera › More › Pro Video

## Why

The S25 Ultra sensor is professional grade — Pro Video unlocks its full potential.

## Steps

1. Open Camera app
2. Swipe to More modes
3. Tap Pro Video
4. Explore ISO, shutter, and log format options

> 💡 **Tip:** Log format (S-Log) preserves dynamic range for color grading in post.
