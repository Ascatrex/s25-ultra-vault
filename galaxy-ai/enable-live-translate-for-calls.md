# Enable Live Translate for calls

> **Path:** Settings › Galaxy AI › Call assist › Live translate

## Why

Essential for international calls, supplier conversations, and customer support in Israel.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Galaxy AI (top-level section in One UI 7)
3. Tap Call assist
4. Tap Live translate
5. Toggle on and select your languages

> 💡 **Tip:** Works on both regular calls and select VoIP apps. Both parties hear a translated voice.
