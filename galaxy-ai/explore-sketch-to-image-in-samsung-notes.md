# Explore Sketch to Image in Samsung Notes

> **Path:** Samsung Notes › Create note › Draw › AI tools › Sketch to image

## Why

Great for quick design concepts, social media content, and visual ideation.

## Steps

1. Open Samsung Notes
2. Create a new note and tap Draw
3. Draw a rough sketch with the S Pen
4. Tap AI tools (sparkle icon)
5. Tap Sketch to image and select a style

> 💡 **Tip:** Try "Watercolor" or "3D cartoon" styles for best results.
