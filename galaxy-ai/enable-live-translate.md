# Enable Live Translate

> **Path:** Settings › Galaxy AI › Call assist › Live translate

## Why

Helps S25 Ultra owners communicate with others who speak different languages

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Galaxy AI
3. Tap Call assist
4. Toggle Live translate on
5. Confirm the setting is enabled

> 💡 **Tip:** Make sure to test the feature after enabling it
