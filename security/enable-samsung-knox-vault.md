# Enable Samsung Knox Vault

> **Path:** Settings › Security and privacy › Samsung Knox

## Why

Your biometrics and credentials cannot be extracted even if the OS is compromised.

## Steps

1. Open Settings
2. Tap Security and privacy
3. Tap Samsung Knox
4. Review Knox Vault status — it is enabled by default on S25 Ultra

> 💡 **Tip:** Knox Vault is always on — this step is to verify it is active and review your protection level.
